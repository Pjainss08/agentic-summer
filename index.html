Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this for?

Builders, designers, founders, and anyone excited about Agentic AI. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, there's a track for you.

Do I need prior AI experience?

No. Events range from beginner-friendly workshops to advanced build sprints. You'll find the right level for you.

Are events paid?

Most events are free to attend. Some premium workshops may have a nominal fee to cover materials and venue costs.

Can I host or partner?

Yes! We're actively looking for community hosts and partners across India. Reach out through the Register section to get started.

What are the benefits of partnering?

Partners get brand visibility, access to top AI talent, speaking opportunities, and direct engagement with the builder community.

How do I apply to host an event?

Fill out the registration form with your city, venue details, and proposed format. Our team will review and get back to you within a week.

What criteria must I meet to be considered?

You need a venue that fits 50+ people, a track record of organizing community events, and genuine enthusiasm for AI and building.
